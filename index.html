About

Who I am

I'm Aswin D Menon, an aspiring aerospace engineer currently honing my skills at IIT Kharagpur. My research interests center around advanced control systems for aircraft and helicopters, incorporating bio-inspired principles and exploring the potential of autonomous navigation. I'm particularly fascinated by the intersection of control theory and fluid dynamics, and I'm eager to contribute to the development of next-generation aircraft that are safer, more efficient, and capable of complex maneuvers. My current research focus involves exploring novel control algorithms for enhanced stability and maneuverability, while also investigating the application of biomimicry to improve aerodynamic performance. I believe that the future of flight lies in intelligent automation, and I'm dedicated to advancing the field through rigorous research and innovative design.
